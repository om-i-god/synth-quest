# SYNTH QUEST — Opening Cutscene

*Pre-game cinematic. Plays before character creation, accessible from the title screen as "Prologue." Advance one panel at a time on a button press. Tape saturation runs continuously beneath every cue.*

---

## PANEL 1
[image]: full black screen, faint dithered noise drifting like dust motes; a single low-luminance line wavering across the middle.
[audio]: deep room-tone hush. master tape hiss in foreground. nothing pitched yet.
[narration]:
Long before the cities,
before the bell-orchards
and the iron mountains —

## PANEL 2
[image]: the wavering line begins to brighten and steady. a single point of light at its center, blooming.
[audio]: a single sustained tone fades in, dead-center, no harmonics. tape wow audible.
[narration]:
there was a sound that
no one was alive to hear.

## PANEL 3
[image]: the point of light fills the screen as a soft circular glow; dithered halo. the line is now a held horizon.
[audio]: the tone deepens, gains a slow body of overtones. drone settles.
[narration]:
It was vast. It was patient.
It contained every voice
that would ever exist.

## PANEL 4
[image]: the glow trembles, then fractures — a slow prism-split into four soft beams angling outward.
[audio]: the single tone splits into four slowly-detuning partials. shimmer. tape flutter widens.
[narration]:
And then, for reasons no
cosmologist has explained,
the First Tone opened.

## PANEL 5
[image]: four glyphs resolve at the ends of the four beams — a circle (sine), a soft-cornered triangle, a hard-edged square, a rippling scan-line (wavetable). dithered, low-key.
[audio]: each partial articulates into its own timbre as its glyph appears. four distinct voices, held.
[narration]:
Four partials endured.
Four Crystals, holding the
world between their voices.

## PANEL 6
[image]: a wide low pixel-vista — terraced valley, distant domed cloister, bell-orchards in concentric rings. mist.
[audio]: triangle pad swells, soft chorus, long hall reverb. faint distant bells on the bar.
[narration]:
The gentle Triangle —
breathing in the cloister
of Hemmelfeld.

## PANEL 7
[image]: a vault interior — narrow, glass-lined corridor receding to a single point of light at the far end.
[audio]: dry sine, no reverb, ringing alone. one perfect partial.
[narration]:
The bright Sine —
alone in the deep vaults
of the Arx Sinaeum.

## PANEL 8
[image]: silhouettes of suspended anvils above a forge-temple; sparks falling on a tight grid.
[audio]: square-wave pulse on the bar. sub-bass thud. crisp tape-saturated low end.
[narration]:
The steady Square —
the heartbeat the cities
walk to without knowing.

## PANEL 9
[image]: a small lamplit barge drifting through a marsh; one stage, one figure with an instrument.
[audio]: detuned reed-organ pad, slow position drift, wavetable scan in the high register.
[narration]:
The shifting Wavetable —
the only Crystal that
travels.

## PANEL 10
[image]: the four glyphs again, side by side — but each is flickering, dim, edges eaten by hiss.
[audio]: each partial drops a few dB, regains, drops further. the tape hiss rises around them.
[narration]:
Tonight, all four
are flickering.

## PANEL 11
[image]: a wheat field at dusk; a child in foreground, mouth open as if singing, but no waveform around her.
[audio]: a thin, half-pitched child's voice, drifting a half-step under. no body. no warmth.
[narration]:
The wheat ripens but does
not satisfy. Children sing
a half-step under pitch.

## PANEL 12
[image]: deep interior — a vast spire seen from inside; a small figure at a console, lit from below by a turning reel of tape. her back to us.
[audio]: a single perfectly-tuned sustained chord. no vibrato. no envelope. it does not move.
[narration]:
At the world's center,
in the Coda, a woman
holds a chord.

## PANEL 13
[image]: closer on the console — two great spools of tape. one of them turning the wrong way. dithered glow.
[audio]: tape reversal whine, slow, unsettlingly tonal. the held chord beneath, unmoving.
[narration]:
The master tape on which
the First Tone was caught
is spooling backward.

## PANEL 14
[image]: the woman's hand on the spool, knuckles pale. on the other channel, a faint ghosted waveform — a voice, almost gone.
[audio]: under the held chord, a thin sine: a voice, nearly inaudible, wavering. tape hiss louder.
[narration]:
She is not finished.
She is listening for
a voice she has lost.

## PANEL 15
[image]: pull back to the cloister at dusk. silhouettes of singers around a domed roof. one silhouette opens its mouth and no waveform comes out.
[audio]: the Triangle Hymn, mid-round — and a voice drops out. then another. the round thins audibly.
[narration]:
And in Hemmelfeld, at dusk,
a six-hundred-year hymn
falters in the round.

## PANEL 16
[image]: four faint sigils kindling at the screen's four corners — sine, triangle, square, wavetable — each with a small dithered figure-shape inside, faces unseen.
[audio]: four small voices, one in each timbre, speak a single syllable each on the bar. the call.
[narration]:
The world is about to need
four voices.

## PANEL 17
[image]: the sigils brighten. the figures inside them turn, as if hearing something far off. centered between them, the Hymn waits — a single triangle tone, holding.
[audio]: the four voices breathe in together. the held chord at the Coda tightens. the hiss waits.
[narration]:
And four voices are about
to be needed.

## PANEL 18
[image]: full black. then, slowly, the title — SYNTH QUEST — etched in dithered letters, breathing in the tape's wow.
[audio]: a single sustained tone. master tape, forward. one bar. then silence with hiss under it. cold open.
[narration]:
The Hymn waits.
